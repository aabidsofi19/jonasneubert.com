Volutabrum.de

Volutabrum.de is a directory of German translations of Latin texts. Sounds like a niche, but isn't. One third of German high school students take Latin, trending up (source). I was one of them, and started this page when I needed it during high school. For a while it was pretty successful with several 100k visits per year, now it's in maintenance mode.
