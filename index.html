My name is Jonas

Hi, I'm Jonas.

I am automation engineer and software developer. Put differently: I like bits and atoms equally. I enjoy working on multi-disciplinary projects that are more complex than complicated, which I currently do at Zymergen.

Previously, I helped build the world's most advanced genomics lab at Counsyl and was graduate student in Hod Lipson's Creative Machines Lab at Cornell University where I worked on modular robotics and programmable matter. I have also been involved in Ignite Ithaca, a postcards thing, a property search engine, a steel factory, a cinema, and a prison teaching program.

Before moving to the New World, I completed an M.Eng. (my undergrad degree) at Imperial College London. Even further in the past, I was born and raised in the country famous for no speed limits and no humor.
